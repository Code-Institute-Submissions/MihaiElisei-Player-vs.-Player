Instructions

  • Press New Game Button;
  • After that it will open a small window where both players should type their names and click on submit button;
  • Game section will appear;
  • Press Start and the Rock Paper Scissors game will decide who start first;
  • For the player who wins Rock Paper Scissors game will appear attack button;
  • After one player attacks the attack button will disappear and will appear for the next player and so on until the health bar will reach 0 for one of the players;
  • When a player is on 0 health the background color for the winner will become green and for the loser red and the Replay button will appear;
  • Whe click on Replay button Rock Paper Scissors game will be played again and game will continue;
  • Score will increase for the winner after every game;
  • When a player will reach 3 score points he will win the game and the match will be over;
  • Name of the winner will be displayed and the Home button that will reset the entire game.

    • Have Fun!
